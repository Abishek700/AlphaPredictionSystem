\section{Main Functions}


The primary function of the system is to assist in identifying potential health issues related to abnormal gait patterns. \\ 
\\ The system analyzes the user’s walking pattern on a flat surface and determines whether the gait is normal or abnormal. The assessment result is displayed via an LED light.
\\ 
\\ 
If an abnormal gait pattern is detected, the system triggers a red LED light. For a normal gait, a blue light is displayed. \\ 
\\ This wearable device is strapped onto the user’s leg, providing insights into potential gait abnormalities, offering an in-home solution that typically requires medical supervision.
