\section{Battery Information}

\begin{itemize}
	\item \textbf{Battery Holder:} \\
	The device is equipped with a coin cell battery holder that holds two 20mm CR2032 batteries, providing a nominal 6V with approximately 220 mAh total capacity.
	
	\item \textbf{Runtime:} \\
	The device is expected to run for no less than 4 hours in active mode and up to 4 months in sleep mode.
	
	\item \textbf{Low Battery Indicator:} \\
	If the power LED (LED 1) dims or flickers, it indicates that the battery charge is around 40\%. If the orange power LED (located in the middle of the board, close to the button—refer to the picture) lights constantly with an orange light, it indicates the battery level is below 25\%.
	
	\item \textbf{Battery Replacement:} \\
	To replace the batteries, refer to Section 7.1.
