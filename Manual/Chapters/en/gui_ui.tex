\section{Getting Started}

\subsection{Step 1: Activating the Device}

\begin{enumerate}
	\item \textbf{Turn On the Device:} \\
	The device comes with the battery already installed. Slide the power switch to the ON position. A green light, located on the left side of the USB port at the bottom of the dashboard, indicates the device is powered and ready for use. If the green LED does not light up, please refer to the Troubleshooting section.

	\end{center}
	\begin{center}

	\end{center}
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Step 2: Attaching the Device}

\begin{enumerate}
	\item \textbf{Secure the Device to Your Foot:} \\
	Use the adjustable strap provided to securely fasten the device to the top of your right  foot. Ensure the strap is snug but comfortable, allowing for natural movement. 
	\begin{center}
		\includegraphics[width=1\textwidth]{Images/strapholes.jpg} % Adjust the path and width as needed
	\end{center}
	\item \textbf{Secure the Device to Your Foot:} \\
	Position the device as shown in the provided photo, with the USB port at the button facing  toward your leg. Make sure the device is securely fastened and seated tightly.
	\begin{center}
		\includegraphics[width=0.7\textwidth]{Images/wearsensor.png} % Adjust the path and width as needed
	\end{center}
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Step 3: Test}

\begin{enumerate}
	\item \textbf{Performing the  Test:} \\
	Rotate around your axis counterclockwise. The device will automatically begin monitoring your gait pattern.
	\begin{center}
		\includegraphics[width=0.8\textwidth]{Images/positiononleg.png} % Adjust the path and width as needed
	\end{center}
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Step 4: Visual Feedback and Alerts}

The device provides real-time visual feedback based on the gait pattern analysis through an LED located in the middle of the dashboard, close to the power button. Refer to the provided photo and sketches for the exact LED location.

\begin{itemize}
	
	\item \textbf{Blue LED}: Indicates normal gait.
